Straky Games Inc. is a little organisation of
creation of video games created by $traker (myself).

She concists of developping games of type Computers or Phone with
multiples langages of programmation (C++, Python, Java, etc...) and/or
softwares of developping (Clickteam Fusion, Game Maker Studio, Unity 3D, etc...)

Some beta testers will be there to test the games before their deployments on
different download or purchase platforms (Steam, Google Play, etc...).
They will give their opinions on the different games tested, so these will be
improved with their tests and their various evaluations.

My games, in this organization are available
in the tab "My Projects" of this website.
